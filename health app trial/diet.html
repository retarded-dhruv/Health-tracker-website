Diet Plans for Body Types

Ectomorph Diet Plan

Ectomorphs typically have a fast metabolism and may find it challenging to gain weight. Your diet should focus on increasing calorie intake, including lean proteins, complex carbs, and healthy fats.

Mesomorph Diet Plan

Mesomorphs have a well-balanced body type, making it easier to build muscle. Your diet should include a mix of lean proteins, carbohydrates, and healthy fats to support muscle growth and recovery.

Endomorph Diet Plan

Endomorphs tend to have a slower metabolism and may struggle with weight management. Focus on a diet with controlled portions, emphasizing lean proteins, vegetables, and complex carbs, while reducing sugar and processed foods.
